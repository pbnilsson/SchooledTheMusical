An original musical fusing classical, hip-hop, Latin, pop, jazz, and gospel, Schooled begins and ends in the same classroom — first with joy, then with wisdom — tracing a brother, a sister, and a friend through a decade of consequence as they discover how to navigate a world changing rapidly around them.

What’s Next for Schooled

Schooled has a book and score in active development and extraordinary audience affirmation for its work so far. We’re now seeking creative and producing partners — including a director and music director — to help bring the full show to life.
