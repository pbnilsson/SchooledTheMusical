From the SXSW EDU Reading

An original musical fusing classical, hip-hop, Latin, jazz, and gospel, Schooled begins and ends in the same classroom — first with joy, then with wisdom — tracing a brother, a sister, and a friend through a decade of consequence as they discover how to navigate a world changing rapidly around them.
