The Show

Who you are,
who you dream to be,
and what you can and cannot learn in school.

Starting with the thrill of the first day of class, Act I follows their adolescent years amidst turbulent times — classes, romance, a letter that goes viral, a rally that ends in gunfire, and the surprising, often humorous work of growing together. Act II jumps ahead ten years, tracing how those years shape their early adulthood, and how their dreams grow, shift, and collide with reality. Told through a joyous blend of classical, contemporary, and hip-hop, it’s a show about awe, ambition, and justice.

Schooled is the first in a trilogy.
