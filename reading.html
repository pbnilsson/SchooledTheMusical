Reading List

Creating a musical is an epic project — and one best entered with eyes wide open. So, since first deciding to develop this project, I’ve been diving deeply into the literature of musical theater to try to give myself the best self-directed MFA I can, reading and watching: shows, craft books, librettos, biographies, and more. This is a running list of those texts I’ve been reading since beginning this show. I’m always looking to learn more, so please send along recommendations!— Peter
