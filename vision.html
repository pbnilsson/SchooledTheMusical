The Vision

One school. Three vantage points. Three individual shows.

I
The Students

Three central characters from their school years through a shooting and into adulthood. The letter, the rally, the decade after — told from inside the experience of being young and trying to change the world.

In active development
II
The Teachers

The same events from the faculty perspective: the lives of a pair of young teachers, the teacher lounge, and the faculty meetings. The professional risks, the personal stakes, the things invisible to the students.

Architecture in place
III
The Head of School

The solitary head of school — her background, her experience during the events of the first two shows, her resignation, and her return through her granddaughter. The show that subverts the assumptions the prior audiences have built.
